The following task records your behavior under distracting conditions. You will be presented two pictures in very quick succession. The first picture is a picture of an animal.

The second picture is that of a Chinese character. After that, a picture with a black and white pattern will be shown. The succession of the pictures is very quick. Please focus on the second picture with the Chinese character.
Your task is to decide whether you find the shown Chinese character more or less appealing than an average Chinese character.

If you find the Chinese character rather unappealing, press [Q]. If you find the Chinese character rather appealing, press [P].

Please notice that there are no right or wrong answers. Decide according to your immediate feelings and as fast as possible.
